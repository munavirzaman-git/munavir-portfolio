About Me

I'm a Robotics Software Engineer based in Calicut, Kerala, holding an M.Tech in Robotics and Automation from the College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET) — one of Kerala's most prestigious technical institutions. My core expertise lies at the intersection of control systems design, sensor fusion, and autonomous navigation.

At IIT Palakkad's Technology Innovation Hub, I spent 1.3 years as an R&D Engineer building the full autonomous navigation stack for an agricultural tractor platform in collaboration with Mahindra & Mahindra and CDAC Hyderabad. I designed and benchmarked five path tracking controllers — Stanley, Pure Pursuit, LQR, MPC, and PID — and built a full EKF sensor fusion pipeline in real agricultural field conditions.

I also completed an internship at Asimov Robotics, Kochi — one of Kerala's best-known robotics companies, recognised for their humanoid and service robot platforms deployed across hospitals and public venues in India. This gave me direct exposure to production-grade robotic software.

I thrive on building complete robotic systems that are robust in real unstructured environments, where theory alone is never enough.

Key Responsibilities

  • Integrated GPS/GNSS for absolute outdoor positioning with local odometry bridging between fixes.

  • Validated navigation pipelines in Gazebo simulation before field deployment.
Technical Contributions

  • Deployed SLAM (GMapping) for mapping and AMCL for probabilistic localisation on the tractor platform.
  • Built modular ROS node architecture for sensor input, controller output, logging, and diagnostics.

Featured Projects

// PROJECT 01 · FEATURED
Ackermann Steering Robot — Full Autonomy Stack
LiDAR + IMU + Wheel Odom → EKF Fusion → SLAM / AMCL → A* Path Planning → Multi-Controller Comparison → Obstacle Detection

A complete autonomous navigation stack on an Ackermann-geometry robot — covering the full pipeline from multi-sensor EKF fusion and SLAM mapping, to A* planning, LiDAR obstacle detection, and a comprehensive five-controller design and benchmarking study.

Sensor Fusion & Localisation
  • EKF fusing LiDAR scan matching, IMU, and wheel odometry for robust continuous pose estimation.
  • GMapping SLAM for simultaneous mapping and localisation in unknown environments.
  • AMCL particle filter for global localisation on a prior map.
  • GPS/GNSS outdoor positioning with local odometry bridging.
Controller Design & Benchmarking
  • Stanley — heading + cross-track error front-axle law.
  • Pure Pursuit — adaptive lookahead by speed and curvature.
  • LQR — optimal state-space control on lateral + heading error.
  • MPC — constrained receding horizon with bicycle model (OSQP).
  • PID — classical cross-track error baseline.
  • Benchmarked on cross-track error, heading error, settling time, and CPU cost.
Path Planning & Obstacle Detection
  • A* algorithm on occupancy grid with dynamic re-planning capability.
  • Real-time LiDAR obstacle detection with safety cost-field integration.
