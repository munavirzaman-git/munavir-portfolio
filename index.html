01 // Profile

About Me

I'm a Robotics Engineer holding an M.Tech in Robotics and Automation from the College of Engineering Trivandrum. My work focuses on building complete autonomous robotic pipelines — tying together perception, motion planning, and real-time control into deployable systems.

At IIT Palakkad, I spent 1.3 years as an R&D Engineer working on an autonomous agricultural tractor in collaboration with Mahindra & Mahindra and CDAC Hyderabad. This gave me deep exposure to kinematic modeling, trajectory generation, path tracking controllers, and sensor fusion in real-world environments.

My technical interests span autonomous navigation, learning-based perception with YOLOv8, constrained MPC implementation in Python and C, and UAV control architectures. I'm driven by the challenge of making robots robust and reliable in unstructured environments.

Key Responsibilities
  • Designed kinematic and dynamic models for tractor motion including Ackermann steering geometry and nonholonomic constraints.

  • Simulated full navigation pipelines in Gazebo before field deployment; validated against real-world test data.

  • Achieved real-time detection throughput with modular, reusable ROS node architecture.
